Our Story

Rooted in the
Soils of Ceylon

Ceylon Coco Creations was born from a simple belief — that the world deserves the purest coconut nature can offer. Our products trace their origins to the fertile coastal farms of Sri Lanka, where coconut palms have flourished for centuries, nurtured by tropical rains and Indian Ocean breezes.

We work hand-in-hand with small-scale farming families, paying fair prices and investing in sustainable practices that preserve the land for future generations. Every product we ship to the UK and the Middle East carries with it the care, craft, and character of the island we call home.

Our Commitment

Growing Good,
Leaving Less

Eco-Friendly Practices

Biodegradable packaging, solar-powered processing units, and carbon-offset shipping partnerships keep our footprint as light as our products.

Supporting Farmers

We pay above fair-trade rates, provide access to agronomic training, and co-invest in community wellbeing initiatives across our partner villages.

Ethical Production

Our processing facilities maintain safe working conditions, gender-equitable hiring, and zero-waste manufacturing protocols.

Ocean & Land Care

A portion of every sale funds coastal reforestation and mangrove restoration projects along Sri Lanka's Western Province.

Zero-Waste Commitment

Coconut husks, shells, and by-products are repurposed into activated carbon, coir fibre, and compost — ensuring nothing from our harvest goes to waste.
